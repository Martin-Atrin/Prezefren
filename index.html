Powerful Features

Everything you need for professional voice transcription and translation

Multi-Engine Transcription

Choose from Whisper.cpp (local), Apple Speech Recognition (on-device), or AssemblyAI (cloud streaming) with sub-500ms latency.

Multiple Floating Windows

Create multiple floating subtitle windows with customizable fonts (up to 72pt), templates, and word-by-word animations.

Intelligent Translation

Dynamic language support: 99 languages with Whisper, 30+ with Apple Translation (macOS 15+), plus Google Gemini fallback.

Advanced Audio Modes

Mono, Goobero (dual-channel with VAD), and Single Earbud modes. Independent L/R channel transcription with anti-hallucination technology.

Record-to-File

High-quality audio recording with native sample rate support (44.1kHz/48kHz). Save transcription sessions with perfect audio fidelity.

Offline & Online Ready

Works 100% offline with Whisper.cpp and Apple Speech, or enhance with cloud services (AssemblyAI/Gemini). Your choice, your privacy.

About Prezefren

Breaking language barriers, one conversation at a time

Prezefren is a free and open source macOS application optimized for Apple Silicon, providing professional-grade real-time voice transcription that works completely offline or with cloud enhancement. No cost, no subscriptions, no restrictions - if you have a Mac, you can use Prezefren.

Perfect for secure environments, travel, or anywhere internet is unreliable. Start with full offline functionality, then add cloud services when available for enhanced features. Your workflow adapts to your environment, and it's yours forever.
